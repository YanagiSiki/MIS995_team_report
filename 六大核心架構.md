### 專案報告六大核心架構 (Project Report Structure)

1. 研究背景與動機 (Research background and motivation)
2. 研究目標與問題 (What are your research objectives and questions?)
3. 研究模型與變項 (Research model and variables: IV, DV, Moderators or Mediators)
4. 文獻探討與假設發展 (Literature review and Hypotheses development)
5. 研究方法 (Methodology: Survey or experiment, etc.)
6. 測量項 (Measurements)
